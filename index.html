August Electric

Engineering & Commissioning Services for Alberta’s Energy and Infrastructure Sectors

August Electric delivers professional electrical engineering and commissioning management services across Canada. With international project experience and a focus on high-performance delivery, our mission is to support critical infrastructure in energy, utilities, and technology-driven environments.

We are proudly Canadian with over 10 years of engineering experience, both domestic and international.

Core Services

  • Electrical system design for data centers, industrial and energy facilities — MCCs, transformers, switchgear, motors, VFDs, EHT, etc.
  • Integrated system testing (IST) and live commissioning
  • Control systems integration (PLC, DCS, automation)
  • Power distribution, EHT, MCC, and transformer systems
  • Data center commissioning for hyperscale and colocation providers: CRAH, BMS, UPS, generators, ATS, AHU, chillers, etc.
  • Support for EPC, EPCM, and construction-driven projects
  • Commissioning Management (Cx L0, L1, L2, L3, L4, L5, L6)
  • Site Acceptance Testing, Factory Witness Testing, IST (Integrated Systems Testing)
  • Prior clients include Equinix, STT, Vantage, and others, for tenants like AWS, Google, Alibaba, etc.

Industries Served

  • Oil & Gas (upgraders, refineries, utilities)
  • Data centers (hyperscale, cloud, colocation)
  • Industrial facilities & infrastructure projects
